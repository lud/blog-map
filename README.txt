# Blog Map Wordpress Widget

This simple widget allows you to display any of your posts and pages on a map.


## Installation & Configuration

After adding the extension in the administration panel, a new "Blog Map" entry
will show in the administration side menu.

On this administration page, you will be able to configure your widget throug 3
menus :

- Posts & Pages
- Pin Design
- Map Background

### Posts & Pages

This menu displays your published posts and pages. For a post (or page) to be
visible on the map, you must provide a country, a location and enable the post
visibility (click on the eye icon).

You can also change the post icon on the map by clicking the icon (the default
icon is a star).

### Pin Design

Here you can change the size, shape, and colors for the pins displayed on the
map.

### Map Background

Here you can choose a map background form a provider. Most providers provide
different variants of their maps.


## Display on your blog

After having added some posts on your map, you just have to add the widget
through the "Appearance > Widgets" menu and then dragging "Blog Map" to your
desired zone.
